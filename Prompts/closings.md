# Closing Patterns

How to end posts. The ending often makes or breaks engagement.

---

## Punchline Closings
Best for: Story posts, product reviews, observations

**Template:** "[thing] is the best [category] your [common comparison] could never be"

**Examples:**
- "[tool] is the best dev experience your enterprise vendor could never be"
- "[app] is the best assistant your coworker could never be"

**Why it works:** Elevates the subject by creating contrast with something relatable.
